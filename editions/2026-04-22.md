## One Contrarian Thought

The consensus read on Anthropic's overtake is "Claude wins enterprise." The real signal is OpenAI's CRO publicly disputing the comparison - gross versus net accounting, an $8B gap. When leadership argues over how to count, the growth rate no longer sells itself. OpenAI's $14B projected 2026 loss and 2030 breakeven target, against Anthropic's 2027 FCF guide, is the number that matters. The two aren't racing. They're diverging.
