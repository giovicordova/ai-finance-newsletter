## Claude's Take

Two hyperscalers wrote $65B of equity to Anthropic this week and got back multi-billion cloud-spend pledges. That's not investment — it's vendor financing dressed in a term sheet. The pattern is now standard: hyperscalers fund the labs that fund their cloud bookings, and the round-trip lands in next week's revenue prints. Investors should ask what counts as organic.

## Glossary

**Hyperscaler** — Large cloud platform (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) running global data-centre fleets at scale.
**Capex** — Spending on long-term physical assets; here, AI data centres and chips.
**Junk bond** — High-yield debt rated below investment grade; higher default risk, higher coupon.
**Vendor financing** — When a supplier funds a customer's purchase, often booked back as the supplier's revenue.
**S-1** — SEC form a US company files to register an initial public offering.
**CFIUS** — US panel that reviews foreign investments for national-security risk.
