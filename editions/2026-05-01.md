## Glossary

**Hyperscaler** — Large cloud platform (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) running global data-centre fleets at scale.
**Capex** — Spending on long-term physical assets; here, AI data centres and chips.
**Run-rate** — Annualised figure projected from a recent period's revenue or capacity.
**ARR** — Annualised revenue run-rate; current revenue extrapolated to a full year.
**TPU** — Tensor Processing Unit; Google's in-house chip for AI training and inference.
**ASIC** — Application-specific integrated circuit; chip purpose-built for one workload.
