### Stories considered
- Anthropic's ~$30B round at a ~$900B valuation, expected to close this month — chosen for news post. Reporting this week (Bloomberg via Yahoo Finance, plus parallel CNBC/TechCrunch/Axios coverage through May) has Anthropic agreeing terms on a fresh round at a pre-money valuation of about $900B, with four co-leads each putting in ~$2B: Sequoia Capital, Dragoneer, Altimeter and Greenoaks; returning investors include Founders Fund and General Catalyst. Reported as the company's likely last private round before a public listing. This is the dominant developing AI-finance story of the cycle and the natural counterpart to OpenAI's May 22 confidential IPO filing — the two leading labs are now jockeying directly on private valuation.
