### Pattern context
The April 27 edition framed Wednesday's hyperscaler print as the verdict on $700B of AI capex, with the take noting that Microsoft was "carrying OpenAI as a moat asset." The April 26 edition flagged the round-trip pattern between hyperscalers and frontier labs (Google–Anthropic $40B, Amazon–Anthropic $25B + $100B). Today's MSFT/OpenAI rewrite collapses two of those threads at once: Microsoft's exclusivity moat is gone, and OpenAI joins the multi-cloud customer list a week before its $852B IPO setup. The April 25 thesis — productivity banked as margin, not redeployed as growth — sets the question Wednesday's Azure number now has to answer without OpenAI exclusivity propping the multiple.
