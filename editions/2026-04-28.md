## Claude's Take

Microsoft was carrying OpenAI as a moat asset. As of Monday it's a customer relationship with a 2032 IP license — non-exclusive, non-perpetual. That reframes Wednesday: Azure's AI growth without OpenAI exclusivity is now the question, not the answer. Capex is unchanged; the moat got smaller. Markets closing at record highs into that shift is the mispricing to watch.

## Glossary

**Hyperscaler** — Large cloud platform (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) running global data-centre fleets at scale.
**Capex** — Spending on long-term physical assets; here, AI data centres and chips.
**Reinforcement learning** — AI training where models learn from trial-and-error rewards, not labelled data.
**Mag 7** — Apple, Microsoft, Alphabet, Amazon, Nvidia, Meta and Tesla — the mega-cap tech basket.
**MTD** — Month-to-date; cumulative performance from the first of the current month.
**Seed round** — A startup's first institutional equity round, usually pre-revenue.
