## Glossary

**Capex** — Spending on long-term physical assets; here, AI data centres and chips.
**FOMC** — Federal Open Market Committee; the Fed body that sets US interest rates.
**DCF** — Discounted cash flow; valuation method discounting future cash by a chosen rate.
**Agentic workloads** — Compute jobs from AI agents that plan and execute multi-step tasks autonomously.
**Post-money valuation** — Company's value after a funding round, including the cash just raised.
**IG** — Investment grade; corporate debt rated BBB- or higher, low default risk.
