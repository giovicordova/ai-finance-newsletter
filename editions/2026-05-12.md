### Pattern context
The week's capacity / distribution thread now extends into a public-market valuation moment. The May 4–8 editions tracked the supply side getting capitalised in private markets — Nvidia funding Corning's fiber plants for up to $3.2B, CoreWeave's $99.4B backlog with $2B fresh from Nvidia and an $8.5B credit facility, AMD's data-center segment +57% to $5.8B. Cerebras is the first true AI-silicon name to test the public market in this cycle at scale. The +37% bump on proceeds and +83% jump on valuation between the April S-1 and the May 11 amendment, plus 20x-oversubscribed books, are the demand-side price signal. The book closes Wednesday; the first print on Thursday is the cleanest mark-to-market the public side has had on an AI-chip pure play since Nvidia's last decisive guide.
