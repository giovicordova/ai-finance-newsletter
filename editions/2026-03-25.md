── DEEP DIVE ───────────────────────────
The Fed's AI impact assessment is the most important document published this week for anyone in AI-finance. The "net positive" headline will grab attention, but the concentration risk warning is the real story. When two companies — OpenAI and Anthropic — provide the foundation models that hundreds of financial institutions build on, a failure at either company becomes a systemic event. The Fed is essentially flagging that AI model providers should be treated like critical financial infrastructure, akin to clearinghouses or payment networks. If that thinking progresses to formal designation, the regulatory burden on foundation model companies will increase dramatically.
