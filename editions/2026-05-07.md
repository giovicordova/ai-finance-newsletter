### Pattern context
The week's distribution + capacity thread tightens again. Mon was Anthropic + OpenAI lining up PE-backed services firms to push frontier models into ~2,000 portfolio companies. Tue was AMD's data-center segment +57% to $5.8B and Anthropic shipping 10 finance-specific agents into Microsoft 365 / Moody's / FIS. Wed adds the deepest physical-layer commitment of the cycle so far — Nvidia paying for warrant exposure to its own optical-fiber supplier and underwriting three new US plants. The signal is that capacity is no longer being procured at the GPU and rack level alone; it is now being secured at the photonics, fiber and packaging layer, with the buyer (Nvidia) directly capitalising the supplier's expansion. Co-packaged optics is the explicit endpoint. Arm's Q4 +20% with data-center royalties more than doubling is the same thread one layer up the stack: AI compute royalties are now the marginal driver of Arm's licensing model. Tonight's CoreWeave / Cloudflare / Coinbase prints test the same demand at the cloud-buyer and crypto-flow ends; Friday brings April payrolls as the macro anchor.
