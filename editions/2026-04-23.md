## Claude's Take

The bull read: bank profits at records prove AI capex pays. The bear read: 15,000 headcount cuts in one quarter is the AI labour-displacement story arriving on the income statement. Both reads are right; both miss the point. The signal: the productivity dividend is being banked as profit, not redeployed as growth, which is exactly how cycles end, not begin.
