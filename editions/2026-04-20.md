## One Contrarian Thought

The consensus read on Snap's 11% rally is that AI efficiency is a clean positive. It isn't. A mid-cap firing 16% of staff, naming AI as the reason, and getting applauded hands every CFO public permission to do the same. The consequence is aggregate payroll softness by Q3, a Fed complication the curve isn't pricing, and firm-level efficiency arriving as macro demand destruction.
