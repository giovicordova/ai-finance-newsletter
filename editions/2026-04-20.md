## One Contrarian Thought

Consensus after TSMC and ASML: AI capex is finally monetising. MiniMax complicates that. If task-level intelligence commoditises 90% below US pricing — as OpenRouter's ranking argues — the enterprise margin thesis underwriting OpenAI's $852B valuation and Anthropic's October IPO depends on safety and latency moats that token buyers price-check weekly. The gap is closing faster than bankers assume.
