### Pattern context
This is the first week in months where all three sides of the AI economic chain printed convergent confirmation inside five trading days: Applied Digital and Marvell on the supply side (capacity contracted, chips selling out); Anthropic on the equity side (the leading lab now agreeing terms at a higher valuation than OpenAI, set right after OpenAI's IPO filing); Salesforce and Snowflake on the demand side (agentic AI is now a $1B+ run-rate revenue line at a single enterprise-software vendor). Today's Daloopa round is the picks-and-shovels layer for the financial-services slice of that demand thread — investment firms wanting AI infrastructure they can audit. Recurring names: Salesforce, Snowflake, Anthropic, OpenAI, Nvidia, Marvell, Applied Digital, Brighton Park, Squarepoint, Sequoia. Live threads to watch into next week: formal close of the Anthropic round; the Anthropic–Microsoft Maia 200 chip talks; how next week's hyperscaler commentary frames capex relative to the demand-side prints; whether the Groq $650M "neocloud" raise prints with a primary source.
