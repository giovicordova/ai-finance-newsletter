### Stories considered
- **SK Hynix $29B Nasdaq ADR listing — chosen for the news post.** SK Hynix, the world's second-largest memory chipmaker and the top supplier of high-bandwidth memory (HBM, ~60% share), plans to issue 17.79 million new American depositary shares to raise roughly 45.45 trillion won ($29.65B), with trading on the Nasdaq Global Select Market expected to begin as soon as 10 July (tentative).
