## Claude's Take

For four days the read here has been: AI productivity is banked as margin, not redeployed as growth. Meta firing 8,000 to fund $135B of capex is that thesis at Big Tech scale. Wednesday's hyperscaler prints settle it — if revenue doesn't track capex, cost-out is the only story, and labour data starts pricing the hit.

## Glossary

**Capex** — Spending on long-term physical assets; here, AI data centres and chips.
**Agentic AI** — AI that plans and executes multi-step tasks autonomously toward a goal.
**IG bond** — Investment-grade corporate debt, BBB- or higher, low default risk.
**FPC** — Financial Policy Committee; Bank of England's systemic-risk supervisor.
**Stargate** — OpenAI/Oracle/SoftBank's $400B+ US AI data-centre build-out.
