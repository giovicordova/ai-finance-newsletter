## One Contrarian Thought

Cursor's $50B tag is being read as application-layer vindication. I'd watch the multiple: 25x $2B ARR, roughly twice what OpenAI or Anthropic trade at. The thesis requires Cursor keeping its customers as the foundation labs launch directly competing code agents. That's fragile. A wrapper at peak access to someone else's model is a fabulous business right up until the model provider ships the same product.
