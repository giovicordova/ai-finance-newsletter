── DEEP DIVE ───────────────────────────
The 35% AI trading volume figure deserves scrutiny. Unlike the algorithmic trading revolution of the 2000s, which was driven by HFT firms with custom hardware and proprietary algorithms, this wave is powered by commoditised AI tools available to anyone with an API key. The democratisation of AI trading is compressing the alpha available from AI-driven strategies, potentially increasing correlation across funds and amplifying market moves during stress events. The SEC's concern isn't unfounded — correlated AI models acting on the same signals could create systemic risk that traditional circuit breakers weren't designed to handle.
